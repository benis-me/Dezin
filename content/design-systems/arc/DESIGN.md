# Arc

> Category: Consumer & Brand

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Playful but composed: clean light surfaces with one expressive violet, soft rounded
shapes, and a sense of delight held in check by good structure.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f7f5fb, --surface-2 #efeaf7
- Text: --fg #1b1726, --fg-2 #4a4458, --muted #756e85
- Accent: --accent #9b5de5 on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Border: --border #ece7f4, --border-strong #d6cee8

## 3. Typography Rules

A rounded humanist sans; mono for code. Body 16px; display 40–64px / 600 with a touch
of character. Two weights.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: violet primary (pill), or ghost; cards: 1px border, soft shadow on hero tiles.
Inputs: 1px border, focus → --accent. Generous radii.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; container max 1100px; airy, slightly asymmetric compositions. Radius 10 / 14 / 20px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Soft, diffuse shadows on key surfaces; never hard edges. Restraint keeps it from getting toy-like.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: one expressive violet, soft rounding, one playful focal move. Don't: rainbow gradients, glow spam, multiple accents, clutter.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; single column under 640; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #fff / fg #1b1726 / accent #9b5de5 / border #ece7f4. Example: "A playful onboarding: violet pill CTA, soft cards, one delightful illustrative moment."
