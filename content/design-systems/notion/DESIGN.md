# Notion

> Category: Productivity & SaaS

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Calm, document-like, warm. A neutral off-white "paper" canvas, generous reading
measure, and quiet UI that recedes behind content. Friendly but not playful.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Background surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f7f6f3, --surface-2 #efeeec
- Text & content: --fg #37352f, --fg-2 #6b6a64, --muted #9b9a93
- Brand & accent: --accent #2f80ed on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Border & divider: --border #e9e9e6, --border-strong #d6d5d1

## 3. Typography Rules

System UI sans for body; an optional serif for document titles. Comfortable
reading: body 16px / 1.6 / 400; H1 30px / 600; measure 65ch. ALL-CAPS labels at
0.06em. One or two weights.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: subtle --surface-2 fill, radius 6px. Cards: 1px --border, gentle hover
--surface-2; minimal shadow. Inputs: 1px border, focus → --accent. Callouts:
--surface bg with a left rule (not a colored accent border).

## 5. Layout Principles

4px grid; centered reading column max 900px; airy vertical spacing. Radius 4 / 6 / 10px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Mostly flat; light shadow (0 4px 12px rgba(15,15,15,0.1)) on menus and hover cards.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: warm neutrals, generous measure, quiet chrome. Don't: harsh pure-white-on-black,
neon, heavy borders, more than one accent.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 600 / 960px; touch targets ≥24px; single column under 600.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Quick colors: bg #fff / surface #f7f6f3 / fg #37352f / accent #2f80ed. Example: "A
note page on warm paper, serif title, 65ch measure, subtle callouts."
