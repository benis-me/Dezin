# Apple

> Category: Consumer & Brand

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Spacious, serene, premium. Large type, lots of air, soft cool-gray surfaces, and
restrained color. Product-forward: imagery and headline carry the page; chrome
nearly disappears.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Background surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f5f5f7, --surface-2 #ececef
- Text & content: --fg #1d1d1f, --fg-2 #515154, --muted #86868b
- Brand & accent: --accent #0071e3 on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Border & divider: --border #d2d2d7, --border-strong #b8b8be

## 3. Typography Rules

SF-style sans for everything. Big and confident: display 48–80px / 600 / -0.02em;
body 17px / 1.5 / 400; tight headline tracking. Centered hero headlines are
acceptable here. Two weights.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: pill (--radius-pill), --accent fill or tinted --accent on --surface.
Cards: --surface bg, large radius (18px), soft shadow on featured tiles only.
Inputs: 1px border, focus → --accent.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; centered container max 980px; very generous vertical rhythm. Large radii
12 / 18 / 980px(pill).

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Mostly flat with soft, diffuse shadows on product cards (0 12px 40px
rgba(0,0,0,0.1)). Never hard edges.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: air, scale contrast, one blue accent, pill buttons. Don't: dense layouts,
hard shadows, multiple accents, tiny type.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 734 / 1068px; touch targets ≥44px (consumer); stack hero under 734.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Quick colors: bg #fff / surface #f5f5f7 / fg #1d1d1f / accent #0071e3. Example: "A
product hero: 72px centered headline, pill CTA, soft-shadow feature cards."
