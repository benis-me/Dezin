# Claude

> Category: AI & LLM

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Warm, calm, literary. A soft paper canvas, terracotta accent, and comfortable
reading rhythm — an AI product that feels considered rather than techy.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #faf9f7, --surface #f3f1ec, --surface-2 #ebe8e1
- Text: --fg #1f1d1a, --fg-2 #4c4843, --muted #807a70
- Accent: --accent #c2603f on --accent-fg #fffaf6
- Border: --border #e6e2da, --border-strong #cfc8bb

## 3. Typography Rules

A humanist sans for UI, an optional serif for long answers. Body 15–16px / 1.6;
measure 65ch. Two weights; warm, unhurried.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: terracotta primary or --surface ghost, radius 8px. Chat bubbles: soft
--surface fill, generous padding, no harsh borders. Inputs: 1px border, focus → --accent.

## 5. Layout Principles

4px grid; centered reading column max 760px; airy spacing. Radius 6 / 10 / 14px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Mostly flat; a faint shadow on menus only. Warmth comes from color, not shadow.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: warm neutrals, one terracotta accent, roomy reading. Don't: cold pure-white/black, neon, multiple accents, dense chrome.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; single column under 640; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #faf9f7 / fg #1f1d1a / accent #c2603f / border #e6e2da. Example: "A chat UI on warm paper: soft bubbles, terracotta send button, 65ch answers."
