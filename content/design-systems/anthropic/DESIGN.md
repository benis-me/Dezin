# Anthropic

> Category: AI & LLM

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Editorial, considered, institutional-warm. The corporate brand voice (not the chat
product): an ivory paper canvas, a serif display setting big, calm headlines, and a
deep book-cloth clay used sparingly. Wide, structured marketing layouts with squared
corners and a typographic backbone — research-lab gravity rendered in warm neutrals.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Background surfaces: --bg #f7f5ef (ivory), --surface #f0ede4, --surface-2 #e7e3d7
- Text & content: --fg #181613 (slate), --fg-2 #3c3a35, --muted #6f6b61
- Brand & accent: --accent #b8553a (book-cloth clay) on --accent-fg #fdfcf9
- Border & divider: --border #e1ddd0, --border-strong #cdc8b8

Budget: ivory + warm neutrals 90–94%, clay 4–6% (one CTA, a rule, a pull-quote
mark, an underline). Colour is restraint; the serif and the grid carry the design.

## 3. Typography Rules

Serif display (Tiempos Headline / Canela) over a grotesque body (Styrene B / Inter)
— the editorial pairing. Display 44–72px / 400–500 / -0.02em, large and unhurried;
H1 32px serif; body 16–18px / 1.6 / 400 grotesque; measure 70ch for prose. Eyebrow
labels in grotesque, 12px, 0.08em, uppercase. Headlines are the artwork.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: clay primary or ivory ghost + 1px --border-strong; squared, radius 4px.
Cards: --surface, 1px --border, near-square corners, no shadow — defined by rule and
spacing. Pull-quotes: large serif with a clay left-rule. Inputs: 1px border, focus →
clay. Footnotes and captions in --muted grotesque. Dividers are hairline rules.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; container max 1180px; wide editorial columns and a strong baseline rhythm.
Radius 2 / 4 / 8px (squared, structured). Generous section spacing; asymmetric
two-column splits (serif headline + grotesque body); rules separate sections.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Effectively flat. Hierarchy comes from type scale, the serif/grotesque contrast, and
hairline rules — not shadow. One faint shadow on menus only (0 4px 14px rgba(24,22,19,0.08)).

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: ivory canvas, serif display, grotesque body, squared corners, one clay accent,
big calm headlines, rule-based structure. Don't: rounded chat bubbles (that's the
Claude product system), neon, gradients, multiple accents, all-sans display, fake
benchmark numbers, drop-shadow cards.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 960 / 1180px; collapse two-column splits to single column under
780; scale display type down fluidly; keep the 70ch measure; touch targets ≥44px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Quick colors: bg #f7f5ef / fg #181613 / accent #b8553a / border #e1ddd0. Example: "A
research announcement page on ivory: a big Tiempos serif headline, grotesque body at
70ch, a clay left-rule pull-quote, squared ghost button, hairline section rules — no
shadows."
