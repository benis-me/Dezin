# Minimal

> Category: Modern & Minimal

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Quiet cool neutrals with a single soft blue. Even more restrained than most — the
point is calm, even rhythm and nothing competing for attention.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f8f9fa, --surface-2 #eef0f2
- Text: --fg #1a1d21, --fg-2 #4b525a, --muted #757d86
- Accent: --accent #3b6fb0 on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Border: --border #e6e9ec, --border-strong #cdd2d8

## 3. Typography Rules

One clean sans; mono for figures. Body 15px / 1.6; tight display tracking. Hierarchy
by scale + weight; tabular-nums. Two weights.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: soft-blue primary or ghost, radius 8px. Cards: 1px border, no shadow.
Inputs: 1px border, focus → --accent.

## 5. Layout Principles

4px grid; container max 1040px; even, generous spacing. Radius 6 / 8 / 12px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Flat; one soft shadow on overlays only.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: cool neutrals, one soft accent, even rhythm. Don't: warm casts, gradients, shadows on cards, a second accent.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; single column under 640; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #fff / fg #1a1d21 / accent #3b6fb0 / border #e6e9ec. Example: "A calm settings page: cool grays, one soft-blue primary, even spacing, no shadows."
