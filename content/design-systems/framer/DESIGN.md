# framer

> Category: Modern & Minimal

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

High-design, dark, confident. The Framer marketing aesthetic: a near-black canvas,
oversized expressive type, generous rounding, and one electric blue that reads as
pure energy. Editorial scale meets product polish — every section is a stage. Bold
but disciplined; the drama comes from type size and space, not clutter.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Background surfaces: --bg #0a0a0a, --surface #141414, --surface-2 #1f1f1f
- Text & content: --fg #f2f2f2, --fg-2 #a3a3a3, --muted #707070
- Brand & accent: --accent #0099ff on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Status: --success #2ecc71, --warn #f5a623, --danger #ff4d4d
- Border & divider: --border #242424, --border-strong #383838

Budget: black/graphite 85–92%, electric blue 5–10% (one hero CTA, one glow moment).

## 3. Typography Rules

Inter at expressive scale — this system leans on big type. Display 64–96px / 600 /
-0.03em (tight); H1 40px; body 16–18px / 400 / relaxed; eyebrow ALL-CAPS 12px /
0.1em. Generous line-height in body, tight in display. Two typefaces max; mono for
code only.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: --radius (12px) or pill, primary = --accent bg, secondary = --surface-2 +
1px border. Cards: --surface, 1px --border, large --radius-lg (20px). Inputs: 1px
border, focus ring → blue. One restrained blue glow on the primary CTA is on-brand
(a soft shadow, not a rainbow). Badges: pill, --surface-2, 11px.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; container max 1200px; full-bleed sections with tall vertical rhythm.
Alternate one huge headline section with one denser proof section. Radius scale:
8 / 12 / 20 / 999px (rounded, modern).

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Mostly flat; one tasteful blue glow allowed on the hero CTA. Real overlays (nav
dropdown, dialog) use soft shadow. No shadow on in-page cards beyond the border.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: oversized display type, tight display tracking, one electric-blue moment,
generous rounding. Don't: multiple gradients, rainbow glows, more than one accent
hue, pure-black text on the blue, cramped hero type.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; display type clamps down hard on mobile (96→40px);
stack to single column under 768; touch targets ≥44px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Quick colors: bg #0a0a0a / fg #f2f2f2 / accent #0099ff / border #242424. Example:
"A bold dark landing hero, 88px headline, electric-blue pill CTA with a soft blue
glow, 20px-rounded feature cards, generous vertical space."
