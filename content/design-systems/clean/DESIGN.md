# Clean

> Category: Modern & Minimal

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Bright and airy: lots of white, crisp blue accent, rounded-but-tidy components.
Friendly SaaS marketing energy without clutter.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f6f8fb, --surface-2 #eaf0f7
- Text: --fg #11181f, --fg-2 #41505e, --muted #6c7a89
- Accent: --accent #2872fa on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Border: --border #e6ecf3, --border-strong #ccd6e0

## 3. Typography Rules

A friendly geometric sans; mono for code. Body 16px; display 40–64px / 600. Two weights; clear scale.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: blue primary (pill or 10px radius) or outline; cards: 1px border, very soft
shadow on featured tiles only. Inputs: 1px border, focus → --accent.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; container max 1120px; generous section spacing. Radius 8 / 12 / 16px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Mostly flat; restrained soft shadows on key cards and menus.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: airy whitespace, one blue accent, tidy rounding. Don't: gradients on everything, shadow overload, multiple accents.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 880 / 1120px; single column under 640; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #fff / fg #11181f / accent #2872fa / border #e6ecf3. Example: "A bright SaaS landing: white space, blue pill CTA, tidy rounded feature cards."
