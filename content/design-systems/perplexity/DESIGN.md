# Perplexity

> Category: AI & LLM

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Calm, answer-first: soft off-white surfaces, a deep teal accent, and a clean
reading column with cited sources. Reference-like and trustworthy.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #fcfcfb, --surface #f3f4f3, --surface-2 #e9ebea
- Text: --fg #14201f, --fg-2 #44524f, --muted #6e7b78
- Accent: --accent #20808d on --accent-fg #f4fbfb
- Border: --border #e2e6e5, --border-strong #cbd2d0

## 3. Typography Rules

A clean sans; mono for inline code. Body 16px / 1.65; measure 70ch. Quiet headings;
source citations in --muted with tabular-nums.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: teal primary or ghost, radius 8px. Answer block with a left rule; source
chips as small bordered pills. Inputs: 1px border, focus → --accent.

## 5. Layout Principles

4px grid; centered column max 800px; generous line spacing. Radius 6 / 8 / 12px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Flat; light shadow on the search bar and menus only.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: calm neutrals, one teal accent, cited sources. Don't: neon, gradients, multiple accents, cramped lines.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; single column under 640; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #fcfcfb / fg #14201f / accent #20808d / border #e2e6e5. Example: "An answer page: teal accents, a left-rule answer block, bordered source chips."
