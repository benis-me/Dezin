# Kami

> Category: Editorial & Print

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Paper-first and print-grade: a warm cream page, a serif reading face, deep ink text,
and a single classic ink-blue accent. Quiet, bookish, timeless.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #f7f3e9, --surface #f1ece0, --surface-2 #e8e1d2
- Text: --fg #20242c, --fg-2 #474d57, --muted #6f7480
- Accent: --accent #1f3a5f on --accent-fg #f7f3e9
- Border: --border #e2d9c6, --border-strong #cabfa8

## 3. Typography Rules

A serif for body + display (Source Serif / Songti-friendly stack); a sans for labels.
Body 17–18px / 1.7; measure 66ch; ALL-CAPS kickers at 0.1em. Restrained bold.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: thin-ruled or ink-fill, radius 2–4px. Rules and small caps over boxes.
Drop caps welcome on features. Inputs: bottom-rule.

## 5. Layout Principles

Baseline-grid feel; container max 720px for prose, full-bleed figures. Radius 0 / 2 / 4px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Entirely flat — it's paper. No shadows.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: serif reading type, warm paper, one ink accent, generous margins. Don't: shadows, gradients, neon, justify body, more than two faces.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; single column under 640; figures go full-bleed.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #f7f3e9 / fg #20242c / accent #1f3a5f / border #e2d9c6. Example: "A long essay on warm paper: 80px serif title, drop cap, 66ch measure, ink-blue links."
