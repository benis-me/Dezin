# Stripe

> Category: Fintech & SaaS

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Polished, trustworthy fintech: crisp white surfaces, deep-ink navy text, a confident
indigo brand accent, and immaculate spacing. Premium without ornament.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f6f9fc, --surface-2 #eef3f8
- Text: --fg #0a2540, --fg-2 #425466, --muted #697386
- Accent: --accent #635bff on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Border: --border #e3e8ee, --border-strong #cdd5df

## 3. Typography Rules

A clean humanist sans; mono for amounts/codes. Body 15–16px; display 40–64px / 600.
Tabular-nums for currency. Two weights.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: --accent primary (subtle gradient acceptable here only on the hero CTA), or
white + 1px border; radius 8px. Cards: --surface, 1px border, a very soft shadow on featured tiles.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; container max 1080px; generous whitespace and section rhythm. Radius 6 / 8 / 12px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Mostly flat with restrained soft shadows on key cards/menus. Never hard edges.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: precise spacing, tabular currency, one indigo accent. Don't: stock purple→blue hero gradients, invented metrics, clutter.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 880 / 1080px; single column under 640; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #fff / fg #0a2540 / accent #635bff / border #e3e8ee. Example: "A payments dashboard: navy text, tabular amounts, indigo primary, soft-shadow summary cards."
