# Editorial

> Category: Editorial & Print

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Print-grade typography on a warm off-white page. The discipline of a magazine:
dramatic scale jumps, a serif for reading, generous margins, and a single ink-red
for emphasis. Authored, not assembled.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Background surfaces: --bg #fbfaf8, --surface #f4f2ee, --surface-2 #ebe8e2
- Text & content: --fg #1a1714, --fg-2 #4a463f, --muted #837d72
- Brand & accent: --accent #b3261e on --accent-fg #fbfaf8
- Border & divider: --border #e3ded5, --border-strong #cbc4b6

## 3. Typography Rules

A serif (Source Serif / Georgia) for body and display; a grotesque sans for labels.
Dramatic hierarchy: display 56–96px / 600 / -0.02em (3–5× body); body 18px / 1.7 / 400;
measure 66ch; ALL-CAPS kickers at 0.1em. Restrained bold (≤2 phrases / 400 words).
Pull-quotes as typographic interrupts, never boxes.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: text-link or thin-ruled; radius 2px. Cards: hairline rule, no fill, no
shadow. Inputs: bottom-rule only. Drop caps and small caps welcome.

## 5. Layout Principles

Baseline-grid feel; asymmetric columns; container max 720px for prose, full-bleed
for figures. Radius 0 / 2 / 4px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Entirely flat. The page is paper. No shadows anywhere.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: serif reading type, dramatic scale, whitespace-as-hierarchy, one ink accent.
Don't: drop shadows, gradients, callout boxes, justify body, more than two faces.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; single column under 640; figures go full-bleed.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

Quick colors: bg #fbfaf8 / fg #1a1714 / accent #b3261e / border #e3ded5. Example:
"A long-form article: 80px serif title, drop cap, 66ch measure, ink-red pull-quotes."
