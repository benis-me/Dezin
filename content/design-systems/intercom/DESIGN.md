# Intercom

> Category: Productivity & SaaS

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Friendly, conversational product UI: light surfaces, a confident product blue,
rounded message bubbles and approachable controls. Warm but professional.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f5f7fa, --surface-2 #e9eef4
- Text: --fg #1c2b3a, --fg-2 #46586b, --muted #6b7c8e
- Accent: --accent #2d7ff9 on --accent-fg #ffffff
- Border: --border #e4e9f0, --border-strong #ccd5e0

## 3. Typography Rules

A friendly sans; mono for code. Body 15–16px; rounded headings. Two weights; clear and legible.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: blue primary (rounded), or ghost; message bubbles (rounded, user vs agent
fills); cards: 1px border, soft shadow on floating widgets. Inputs: pill or 10px radius.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; container max 1080px; comfortable spacing. Radius 10 / 14 / 18px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Flat in-page; soft shadow on the messenger widget and menus.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: rounded friendly shapes, one product blue, conversational tone. Don't: harsh corners, neon, multiple accents, dense tables-everywhere.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; the messenger collapses to a bubble; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #fff / fg #1c2b3a / accent #2d7ff9 / border #e4e9f0. Example: "A support messenger: rounded bubbles, blue send, soft-shadow floating widget."
