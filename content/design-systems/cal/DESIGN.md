# Cal

> Category: Productivity & SaaS

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Near-monochrome and friendly: white surfaces, a near-black primary, soft rounded
controls. Scheduling-app calm — clear, quiet, and confidence-inspiring.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

- Surfaces: --bg #ffffff, --surface #f7f7f8, --surface-2 #efeff1
- Text: --fg #111114, --fg-2 #45454d, --muted #76767f
- Accent: --accent #1a1a1f on --accent-fg #ffffff (near-black primary)
- Border: --border #e7e7ea, --border-strong #d0d0d6

## 3. Typography Rules

A clean sans; mono for times/dates (tabular-nums). Body 15px; restrained headings. Two weights.

## 4. Component Stylings

Buttons: near-black primary (rounded) or outline; time-slot chips as bordered pills;
cards: 1px border, no shadow. Inputs: 1px border, focus ring. Generous rounding.

## 5. Layout Principles

8px grid; container max 1040px; comfortable spacing; calendar grid aligns to tabular-nums. Radius 8 / 12 / 16px.

## 6. Depth & Elevation

Flat; one soft shadow on popovers (date pickers, menus).

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

Do: monochrome calm, rounded controls, tabular times. Don't: a color accent (stay neutral), gradients, shadows on cards.

## 8. Responsive Behavior

Breakpoints 640 / 1024px; stack the calendar under 640; targets ≥24px.

## 9. Agent Prompt Guide

bg #fff / fg #111114 / accent #1a1a1f / border #e7e7ea. Example: "A booking page: rounded near-black buttons, bordered time-slot chips, tabular times."
