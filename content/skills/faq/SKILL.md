# FAQ

A single `index.html` FAQ that answers fast and doesn't overwhelm.

## Structure

- Group questions into **at most 5 categories** (Miller); show the group labels up top.
- Each item: a clear question (the scannable part) and a concise answer. Disclosure (accordion) is fine — but the most common 1–2 answers can be open by default.
- Order by likelihood: the questions most people actually ask come first (Pareto).
- One search/filter only if there are many items; otherwise skip it.

## Craft

Questions in a slightly heavier weight; answers in body with 60–75ch measure. One accent for
links inside answers. Dividers between items, no cards. Real questions and answers from the brief —
no "Question one / Question two". Keyboard-operable disclosures with visible focus.
