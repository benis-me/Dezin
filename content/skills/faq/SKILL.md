# FAQ

An FAQ answers the real doubts fast. Research the actual questions, structure them by
likelihood, then build.

## Research

- The questions people genuinely ask about this product or topic — from support threads,
  reviews, sales objections, the community. The value is in real questions, not invented
  ones.
- Which one or two answers unblock the most people (Pareto) — those lead.

## Structure

- Group questions into **at most 5 categories** (Miller); show the group labels up top.
- Each item: a clear, scannable question and a concise answer. An accordion is fine — but
  the most common 1–2 answers can be open by default. Order by likelihood.
- Add a search/filter only if there are many items.

## Craft

Questions in a slightly heavier weight; answers in body with a 60–75ch measure. Real
questions and answers from research. Keyboard-operable disclosures with visible focus.
