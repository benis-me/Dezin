# Landing page

A single marketing page that reads as designed, not generated.

## Avoid the AI skeleton

Do not ship Hero → Features → Pricing → FAQ → CTA in order with no variation.
Introduce one unconventional section: a full-bleed quote, a comparison against the
status quo, an inline mini-demo, or a single product detail rendered large.

## Craft

- Flat surfaces plus intentional type beat any gradient. No purple→blue or
  blue→cyan "trust" gradients.
- Monoline SVG icons with `currentColor`, never emoji as feature icons.
- Microcopy with a point of view: "Start tracking" beats "Get started".
- One accent, used twice at most. Borders over shadows.

Pull real product details. If you lack a number, use a labelled placeholder —
never invent one.
